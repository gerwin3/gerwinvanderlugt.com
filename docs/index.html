> Oddity.ai

Oddity.ai is the only commercial solution for actual real-time violence detection on CCTV cameras. Unlike other solutions, it actually works and has a false positive rate of less than 0.1 per camera per day. It is deployed in multiple city centers, transporation hubs, prisons, and more places where there is a risk of violence.
